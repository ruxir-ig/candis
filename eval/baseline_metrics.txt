Interpretation
--------------
* Both qrels are near-saturated at the top, so absolute NDCG movement from new
  rankers will be small. The real signal for the research approaches is:
    (a) the LLM-qrel "buried good candidates" list (15+ grade-3/4 at ranks
        #100-165) — rescuing these is the win condition, and
    (b) manual top-20/top-100 audit quality + the robustness audit (Day 5).
* The LLM rerank improves QUALITATIVE top-100 membership (Google/Netflix/Salesforce
  in; grade-2 services folks out) but is mildly circular vs the LLM qrel and
  slightly reorders against the hand qrel. Treat rule+semantic (A) as the
  non-circular baseline to beat.
* Validation: output/baseline.csv passes validate_submission.py.

Acceptance gates for new rankers (new_plan.md):
  1. composite improves OR stays within a tiny margin
  2. top-10 manual quality improves or stays same
  3. no honeypots/non-technical candidates enter top 100
  4. runtime < 5 min, CPU-only, no network
  5. artifacts documented
Otherwise: ablation-only, not in final.
